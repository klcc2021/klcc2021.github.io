Registration for KLCC is free. However, it is mandatory to register for participating in KLCC 2021. Please register by May 28, 2021.

Click here to register. You will be directed to an EasyChair submission page. Please sign up for EasyChair if you don't already have an account. Once logged in, click on "Start Registration". Then, you'll see "Register yourself" on the upper right corner. Please click on it and fill out the registration form.

You will receive specific instructions on how to participate in KLCC 2021 via email by June 3. Please contact the organizers at klcc2021@cornell.edu if you need further assistance.
